<p><p>Induction cap sealing machines are specialized equipment used to seal the openings of containers, particularly bottles, with a foil liner that provides an airtight seal. This process involves the application of electromagnetic induction to heat a foil liner, which melts the adhesive and forms a secure seal upon cooling.

<p><p>The Autonomous Emergency Braking System (AEB) enhances vehicle safety by detecting potential collisions and automatically applying the brakes if the driver fails to respond in time. The market is categorized into three types: Forward Emergency Braking actively monitors obstacles in the vehicle's path and halts the vehicle to prevent front-end collisions. Reverse Emergency Braking protects against rear-end crashes during reversing maneuvers. Multi-Directional Emergency Braking addresses threats from all directions, providing comprehensive coverage against potential impacts. Together, these systems aim to reduce accidents and improve road safety.</p></p>

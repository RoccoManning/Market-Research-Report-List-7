<p><p>The neurovascular embolization device market encompasses several key types used to treat vascular conditions in the brain. Embolization coils are used to occlude aneurysms or abnormal vessels. Flow diversion stents redirect blood flow away from a target area, promoting healing. Liquid embolic systems involve injectable agents that solidify within vessels to block blood flow. Aneurysm clips are surgical instruments applied to secure aneurysms, preventing rupture. Each type offers distinct advantages for specific conditions and patient needs, enhancing treatment outcomes.</p></p>

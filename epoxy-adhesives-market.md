Two-component adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that need to be mixed before application, offering enhanced bonding strength and durability. Other types may include specialty formulations designed for specific applications or environments, catering to diverse industrial needs, such as heat resistance or flexibility in bonding.</p></p>

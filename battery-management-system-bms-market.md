<p><p>A Battery Management System (BMS) oversees battery performance, ensuring safety, longevity, and efficiency. In the market, three main types are identified: </p><p>1. **Distributed BMS** manages individual battery cells or modules, enhancing scalability and reliability.  </p><p>2. **Centralized BMS** consolidates control in a single unit, simplifying management for smaller systems.  </p><p>3. **Modular BMS** combines aspects of both, enabling flexible configurations for varying applications, allowing systems to be tailored for specific energy storage needs. Each type caters to different operational needs and scale.</p></p>

Line-fit systems are integrated into aircraft during the manufacturing process, allowing for seamless installation and optimized design. Conversely, retro-fit systems are added to existing aircraft post-manufacture, enhancing their capabilities without the need for new models. Both types aim to improve operational efficiency, reduce maintenance costs, and ensure aircraft safety through real-time monitoring of health and performance parameters.</p></p>

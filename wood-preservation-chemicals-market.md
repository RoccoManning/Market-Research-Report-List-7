<p><p>The wood preservation chemicals market encompasses various types designed to protect wood from decay, insects, and environmental damage. Water-borne preservatives, which use water as a carrier, are eco-friendly and easy to apply, making them popular for residential applications. Oil-borne preservatives penetrate deeply, offering long-lasting protection but may require more careful handling due to toxicity. Light organic solvent preservatives combine attributes of both, using low-toxicity solvents for improved penetration and reduced environmental impact, catering to diverse applications in the wood industry.</p></p>

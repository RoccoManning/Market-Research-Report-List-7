<p><strong>The Floating LNG Tanker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Floating LNG Tanker market is categorized into Type I and Type II segments. Type I refers to floating LNG storage and regasification units (FSRUs) that directly convert liquefied natural gas into gas for immediate use, typically employed near shore in smaller markets. Type II involves floating LNG production storage and offloading units (FPSoUs) which extract and liquefy natural gas offshore, storing it for later transport. Both types facilitate flexible and efficient gas supply in diverse locations, enhancing energy accessibility.</p></p>

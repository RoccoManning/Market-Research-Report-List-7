<p><p>The wood-based panels market includes a variety of products, each with unique properties and applications. Particleboard (PB) is made from wood particles and is commonly used in furniture. Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF) offers a smooth surface for cabinetry and moldings. High Density Fibreboard (HDF) is denser, making it suitable for flooring. Oriented Strand Board (OSB) consists of strands of wood bonded together, used mainly in construction. Plywood, made of thin wood layers, is versatile for furniture and structural applications.</p></p>

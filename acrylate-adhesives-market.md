<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic</li><li>Thermosetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylate adhesives are categorized into two main types: thermoplastic and thermosetting. Thermoplastic acrylate adhesives remain flexible when heated, allowing for easy reprocessing and adjustment. They are ideal for applications where flexibility and durability are essential. In contrast, thermosetting acrylate adhesives undergo a chemical change when cured, resulting in a rigid structure with excellent thermal and chemical resistance. This makes them suitable for applications requiring strong, permanent bonds. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>

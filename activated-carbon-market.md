<p><p>The activated carbon market is categorized into three main types: Powder Activated Carbon (PAC), Granular Activated Carbon (GAC), and Others. PAC consists of fine particles and is primarily used for air and water treatment applications due to its high surface area and adsorption capabilities. GAC, on the other hand, features larger granules and is favored for its effectiveness in water purification and industrial processes. The "Others" category includes specialized forms, such as pellets or extruded activated carbon, catering to specific industrial applications.</p></p>

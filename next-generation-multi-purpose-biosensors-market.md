<p><p>Next-Generation Multi-purpose Biosensors encompass various types, each with unique detection mechanisms. Nanomechanical biosensors utilize changes in mechanical properties at the nanoscale to detect biological interactions. Optical biosensors rely on light absorption or fluorescence changes to identify targets. Electrochemical biosensors measure current or potential changes in response to biochemical reactions. Thermistor/calorimetric biosensors analyze temperature variations during reactions. Other biosensors include diverse technologies, expanding applications in healthcare, environmental monitoring, and food safety, enhancing sensitivity and accuracy in detection.</p></p>

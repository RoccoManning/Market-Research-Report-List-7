<p><p>The Sputnik Internet of Things market encompasses various service types, primarily backhaul services and direct-to-satellite services. In contrast, direct-to-satellite services enable IoT devices to communicate directly with satellite systems, bypassing terrestrial infrastructure. This approach ensures global coverage and connectivity in hard-to-reach areas, making it essential for industries such as agriculture, logistics, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>

<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Buzzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buzzers are categorized based on their operating principles: electromagnetic and piezoelectric. Electromagnetic buzzers use a coil and magnetic field to create sound, commonly found in alarms and timers. They provide robust, low-frequency output but can be bulky. Piezoelectric buzzers, on the other hand, utilize piezoelectric materials to produce sound through mechanical stress, resulting in compact designs and higher frequency outputs. Both types serve various applications, including consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial equipment, each with unique benefits and limitations.</p></p>

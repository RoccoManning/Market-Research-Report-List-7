<p><p>The polyurethane non-woven fabric market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Spunbond Non-Woven Fabric**: Created by bonding fibers through heat, ideal for durability.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Spunlace Non-Woven Fabric**: Made by mechanically entangling fibers, offering softness and absorbency.</p><p>3. **Needle Punch Non-Woven Fabric**: Uses barbed needles to interlace fibers, providing strength and thickness.</p><p>4. **Meltblown Non-Woven Fabric**: Formed from extruded molten polymer fibers, excellent for filtration applications.</p><p>5. **Wet Laid Non-Woven Fabric**: Crafted by laying fibers in water, suitable for high-volume applications.</p><p>6. **Other**: This includes emerging or specialized non-woven technologies.</p></p>

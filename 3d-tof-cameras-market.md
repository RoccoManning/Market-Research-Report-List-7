<p><p>The 3D Time-of-Flight (ToF) cameras market consists primarily of CMOS and CCD types. CMOS Time-of-Flight cameras utilize complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, offering advantages like lower power consumption and faster processing speeds. They are widely used in applications such as smartphones and security systems. CCD Time-of-Flight cameras, on the other hand, provide superior image quality and sensitivity, making them ideal for high-resolution imaging applications in medical devices and scientific research. Both technologies serve distinct market needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive radar market is primarily segmented into two frequency bands: 24GHz and 77GHz. The 24GHz automotive radar typically offers shorter-range applications, suitable for functions like parking assist and collision avoidance at low speeds. In contrast, the 77GHz automotive radar is used for long-range applications, enabling features such as adaptive cruise control, lane keeping, and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). The 77GHz band provides higher resolution and better target detection, making it increasingly preferred for modern automotive safety and automation.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Unidirectional ESD Protection Diode</li><li>Bidirectional ESD Protection Diode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrostatic discharge protection diode market is categorized into two main types: unidirectional and bidirectional ESD protection diodes. Unidirectional ESD protection diodes allow current to flow in one direction and are typically used in applications where the polarity is consistent. In contrast, bidirectional ESD protection diodes can handle current flow in both directions, making them suitable for circuits with alternating voltages. Both types play a crucial role in safeguarding electronic components from voltage spikes and transient events.</p></p>

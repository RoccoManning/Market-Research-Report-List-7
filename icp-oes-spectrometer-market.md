<p><p>ICP-OES (Inductively Coupled Plasma Optical Emission Spectroscopy) spectrometers come in two main types: sequential and simultaneous. Sequential ICP-OES analyzers measure one wavelength at a time, providing high sensitivity and resolution for complex samples, but can be slower due to the sequential approach. In contrast, simultaneous ICP-OES instruments analyze multiple wavelengths concurrently, allowing for faster analysis and higher throughput, making them ideal for large-scale testing where time efficiency is crucial. Both types serve diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>Successive Approximation Analog-to-Digital Converters (SAR ADCs) are widely used devices that convert analog signals into digital data by approximating the input voltage through a series of comparisons. SAR ADCs are favored for their high resolution, moderate speed, low power consumption, and simplicity in design, making them suitable for various applications, including industrial automation, consumer electronics, and medical devices.</p><p>The SAR ADC market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-performance data acquisition systems and advances in technology.

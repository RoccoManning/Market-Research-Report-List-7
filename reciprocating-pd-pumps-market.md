<p><p>Reciprocating Positive Displacement (PD) Pumps encompass three main types: plunger pumps, piston pumps, and diaphragm pumps. Plunger pumps utilize a cylindrical plunger to move fluid, suitable for high-pressure applications. Piston pumps employ a piston within a cylinder, offering versatility across various fluids and pressures. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create flow, effective for handling corrosive or viscous substances. Each type provides distinct advantages in efficiency, pressure capability, and fluid compatibility, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The demineralized allografts market consists of three main types: gel, putty, and putty with chips. Demineralized allograft gel is a fluid formulation that facilitates easy application and integration for bone regeneration. Putty, a more viscous option, provides structural support and is moldable, making it ideal for various surgical applications. Putty with chips combines the benefits of both putty and bone chips, offering enhanced osteoconductive properties while maintaining shape, suitable for complex orthopedic procedures and enhancing bone healing.</p></p>

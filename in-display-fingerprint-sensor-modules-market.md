<p><p>The display fingerprint sensor modules market primarily consists of two types: optical and ultrasonic fingerprint modules. Optical fingerprint modules use light to capture fingerprint images, making them suitable for various devices due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, ultrasonic fingerprint modules utilize sound waves to scan fingerprints, offering enhanced security by penetrating the outermost layer of skin and capturing detailed 3D data. Both technologies cater to different security needs and user experiences in mobile and electronic devices.</p></p>

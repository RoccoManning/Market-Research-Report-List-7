<p><p>The bare-metal vascular stents market is categorized into three main types: coronary stents, peripheral stents, and others. Coronary stents are designed for implantation in coronary arteries to alleviate blockages and maintain blood flow to the heart. Peripheral stents are used in arteries outside the heart, often to treat vascular diseases in limbs and other regions. The "others" category encompasses stents designed for specialized applications in various vascular systems, addressing a range of medical conditions involving blood vessels.</p></p>

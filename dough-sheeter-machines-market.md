Tabletop dough sheeters are compact and ideal for small operations, easily fitting onto countertops for limited production scales. Conversely, floor dough sheeters (or large models) are designed for higher volumes, featuring robust structures and wider rollers, making them suitable for larger bakeries and commercial establishments that require substantial dough processing.</p></p>

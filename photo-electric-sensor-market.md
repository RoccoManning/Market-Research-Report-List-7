<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Photo-electric Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-beam Sensors</li><li>Retro-reflective Sensors</li><li>Diffuse-reflective Sensors</li><li>Other Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photo-electric sensor market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Through-beam Sensors** use a direct light beam between transmitter and receiver, offering long-range detection.</p><p>2. **Retro-reflective Sensors** reflect light off a surface back to the receiver, allowing for versatile placement.</p><p>3. **Diffuse-reflective Sensors** detect objects by reflecting light from them back to the sensor, useful for close-range applications.</p><p>4. **Other Types** encompass specialized sensors such as laser sensors and fiber optic sensors, catering to unique industrial needs and applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The bathroom cleaners market includes various types, notably alkali and strong acid cleaners. Alkali cleaners, typically pH-based, effectively dissolve grease, soap scum, and mineral deposits, making them suitable for general cleaning tasks. Strong acid cleaners, on the other hand, contain powerful acids that tackle tough stains, limescale, and rust, particularly in hard water areas. Both types cater to different needs, with alkali cleaners often used for regular upkeep and strong acids reserved for heavy-duty cleaning challenges in bathrooms.</p></p>

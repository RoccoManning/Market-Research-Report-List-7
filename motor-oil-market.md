<p><p>The motor oil market is divided into three main types: ordinary motor oil, synthetic oil, and semi-synthetic oil. Ordinary motor oil, derived from refined crude oil, offers basic lubrication and protection, making it cost-effective but less efficient under extreme conditions. Synthetic oil, chemically engineered for superior performance, provides excellent protection, longevity, and fuel efficiency, albeit at a higher price. Semi-synthetic oil combines both types, offering a balance of performance and cost, catering to customers seeking enhanced protection without the full price of synthetic options.</p></p>

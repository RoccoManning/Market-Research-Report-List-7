<p><p>Hydraulic valves in the excavator market are essential components that regulate fluid flow and pressure. There are three main types: </p><p>1. **Pressure Control Valves** manage system pressure, preventing overload and ensuring safety. </p><p>2. **Flow Control Valves** regulate the speed of hydraulic actuators by controlling fluid flow rates. </p><p>3. **Directional Control Valves** direct fluid to different functions of the excavator, enabling precise movement and operation. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing excavator performance and efficiency.</p></p>

<p><p>E-beam evaporator systems are crucial in thin film deposition for various industries. The market includes multi-pocket e-beam sources and single pocket e-beam sources. Multi-pocket e-beam sources allow for simultaneous evaporation of multiple materials, enhancing efficiency and flexibility in production. In contrast, single pocket e-beam sources focus on one material at a time, offering greater precision but less versatility. Both types cater to different production requirements, balancing factors like deposition rate, material diversity, and application specificity in thin film technology.</p></p>

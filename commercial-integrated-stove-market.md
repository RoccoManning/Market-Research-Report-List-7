<p><p>The Commercial Integrated Stove market features various types, including the Deep Well and Side Suction stoves. Deep Well stoves are designed for high-efficiency cooking in large volumes, utilizing deep compartments to hold pots. They excel in traditional cooking methods and are often used in restaurants and food service settings. On the other hand, Side Suction stoves incorporate a ventilation system that draws smoke and odors away from the cooking area, making them ideal for open kitchen environments and improving air quality.</p></p>

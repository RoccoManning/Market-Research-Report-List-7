<p><p>Heat therapy pumps in the market are categorized into two main types: tap water and pure water. Tap water heat therapy pumps utilize standard municipal water, providing an effective, cost-efficient solution for heat treatment. They are widely accessible and suitable for general applications. In contrast, pure water heat therapy pumps use distilled or deionized water, ensuring higher purity and reducing the risk of impurities interfering with heat transfer, making them ideal for specialized therapies and sensitive applications.</p></p>

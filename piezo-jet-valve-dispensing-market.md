<p><p>The Piezo Jet Valve Dispensing Market is categorized based on jet frequency, which determines the speed and precision of dispensing materials. Jet frequency below 500Hz is suitable for thicker materials, offering controlled application. Frequencies between 500-1000Hz are versatile, balancing speed and viscosity. The 1000-1500Hz range is ideal for medium viscosities, achieving faster dispensing rates. Frequencies above 1500Hz cater to thin fluids, enabling rapid and fine dispensing, essential for high-speed production processes in various industries like electronics and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>

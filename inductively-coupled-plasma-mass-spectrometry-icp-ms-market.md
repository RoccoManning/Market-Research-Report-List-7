ICP-MS is widely utilized in environmental monitoring, food safety, pharmaceuticals, and geological studies due to its high sensitivity and precision.</p><p>The ICP-MS market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for accurate and rapid analysis in various applications.

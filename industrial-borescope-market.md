<p><p>The Industrial Borescope market is primarily segmented into flexible and rigid borescopes. Flexible borescopes feature a flexible insertion tube, allowing for access to complex or tight spaces, making them ideal for inspecting intricate machinery and structures. Rigid borescopes, on the other hand, offer a straight, inflexible design suited for direct visual inspections in larger, more accessible areas. Both types serve critical roles in maintenance, quality control, and diagnostics across various industries, including aerospace, manufacturing, and automotive.</p></p>

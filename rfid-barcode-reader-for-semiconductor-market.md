<p><p>RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) and barcode readers are essential in the semiconductor market for inventory and asset tracking. The CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) method uses an array of light sensors to read barcodes by capturing reflected light, offering quick scanning and high accuracy. In contrast, the laser method employs a focused beam of light to decode barcodes from a distance, making it suitable for high-speed environments. Both technologies streamline processes, improve efficiency, and enhance supply chain management in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>

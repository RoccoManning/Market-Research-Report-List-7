<p><p>PVD coating/plating machines utilize physical vapor deposition techniques to create thin films on various substrates. The evaporation coating machine heats materials to form vapor that condenses on the substrate, providing a uniform layer. Sputtering film coaters use energetic ions to dislodge material from a target, depositing it on the substrate. Other market types may include pulse laser deposition or hybrid systems that combine multiple techniques, catering to diverse applications across industries like electronics, optics, and automotive.</p></p>

<p><p>Polymer track-etched membranes are classified into three main types based on the material used: Polycarbonate (PC) type, Polyester (PET) type, and Polyimide (PI) type. The PC type is known for its high optical clarity and rigidity, making it ideal for filtration applications. PET type offers superior chemical resistance and thermal stability, suitable for various industrial processes. PI type features excellent thermal stability and mechanical strength, often used in advanced applications like microelectronics and biotechnological research. Each type serves distinct applications across multiple industries.</p></p>

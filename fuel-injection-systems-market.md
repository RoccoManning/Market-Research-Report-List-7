<p><strong>The Fuel Injection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI)</li><li>Mechanical. Fuel Injection System (MFI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel injection systems market comprises two primary types: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) and Mechanical Fuel Injection (MFI). EFI uses electronic control units to precisely manage fuel delivery, enhancing efficiency and performance in modern engines. It offers real-time adjustments based on engine conditions. In contrast, MFI relies on mechanical components to inject fuel, typically found in older or simpler engines. While MFI is generally less efficient and may provide less precise control, it remains relevant in specific applications where simplicity and cost-effectiveness are prioritized.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Collectors</li><li>Frothers</li><li>Dispersants</li><li>Activators</li><li>Depressants</li><li>Flocculants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mining flotation chemicals market comprises various agents essential for the mineral separation process. Collectors enhance mineral surface hydrophobicity to facilitate attachment to bubbles. Frothers stabilize bubble formation to improve floatation efficiency. Dispersants prevent particle agglomeration, ensuring even distribution. Activators enhance the effectiveness of collectors for specific minerals, while depressants selectively inhibit certain minerals from floating. Lastly, flocculants promote sedimentation of fine particles for efficient separation. Together, these chemicals optimize flotation processes in mineral extraction.</p></p>

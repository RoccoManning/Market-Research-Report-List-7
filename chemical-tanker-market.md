Coastal chemical tankers (5,000-9,999 DWT) operate along coastlines, facilitating short to medium-haul shipments. Deep-sea chemical tankers (10,000-50,000 DWT) engage in international trade, carrying large quantities of bulk chemicals across oceans. Each type plays a crucial role in the overall supply chain of chemical distribution.</p></p>

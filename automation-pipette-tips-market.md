<p><p>The automation pipette tips market is divided into two main types: non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are commonly used for general liquid handling tasks, allowing for efficient and quick sample transfers without contamination risks. In contrast, filtered pipette tips feature a built-in filter that prevents aerosol contamination and protects the pipette from damaging harsh materials. These tips are essential in sensitive applications, such as molecular biology and microbiology, where sample integrity is critical.</p></p>

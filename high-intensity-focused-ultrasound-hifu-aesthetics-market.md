<p><p>The High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) aesthetics market is divided into two primary types: Ultrasound-Guided and MR-Guided. </p><p>Ultrasound-Guided HIFU utilizes real-time ultrasound imaging to target specific tissue areas precisely, enhancing treatment accuracy for skin tightening and lifting. </p><p>In contrast, MR-Guided HIFU employs Magnetic Resonance Imaging to provide detailed anatomical visualization, allowing for a thorough understanding of tissue structures and improving the efficacy of non-invasive procedures. Both types focus on enhancing cosmetic outcomes with minimal downtime.</p></p>

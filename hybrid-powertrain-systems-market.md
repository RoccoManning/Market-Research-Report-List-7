<p><strong>The Hybrid Powertrain Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Series Hybrid</li><li>Series-parallel Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid powertrain systems are categorized into three main types: parallel hybrid, series hybrid, and series-parallel hybrid. In parallel hybrids, both the internal combustion engine and electric motor can power the vehicle simultaneously. Series hybrids rely solely on the electric motor, with the internal combustion engine acting as a generator to produce electricity. Series-parallel hybrids combine both approaches, allowing for flexible operation using either or both power sources. Each type offers distinct advantages in efficiency and performance, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>

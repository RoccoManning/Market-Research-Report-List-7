<p><p>A Tailgating Detection System (TDS) is designed to monitor and prevent unauthorized access in secure areas by detecting individuals closely following one another. The market comprises two main types: Imaging Measurement Systems and Non-Imaging Systems. Imaging Measurement Systems utilize cameras and advanced image processing technologies for accurate detection and identification of individuals. In contrast, Non-Imaging Systems rely on sensors and algorithms to monitor movements and establish proximity, providing effective tailgating detection without the need for visual data.</p></p>

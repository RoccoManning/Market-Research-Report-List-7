<p><p>The vegetable protein market encompasses various types, each with unique properties. Wheat protein, extracted from gluten, is commonly used for its elasticity in food applications. Soy protein concentrate retains some fat and carbohydrates, offering a versatile ingredient for various products. Soy protein isolate is highly refined, boasting a high protein content, while textured soy protein mimics meat, making it popular in meat alternatives. Pea protein, derived from yellow peas, is increasingly favored for its allergen-friendly profile and excellent nutritional value.</p></p>

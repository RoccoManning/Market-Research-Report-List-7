<p><strong>The Calorimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Scanning Calorimeter</li><li>Oxygen Bomb Calorimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calorimeter market encompasses various types, including Differential Scanning Calorimeters (DSC) and Oxygen Bomb Calorimeters. DSC measures heat flow associated with material transitions, providing insights into thermal properties, phase changes, and stability. It is widely used in pharmaceuticals, polymers, and materials science. Conversely, Oxygen Bomb Calorimeters are designed for measuring the heat of combustion in materials, making them essential for fuel analysis, food science, and energy production studies. Both instruments play crucial roles in material characterization and energy efficiency assessments.</p></p>

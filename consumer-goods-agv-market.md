<p><p>The Consumer Goods AGV market comprises three main types: Automated Forklift, Unit Load, and Tugger AGVs. Automated Forklift AGVs are designed for lifting and transporting pallets and heavy goods autonomously, ideal for warehouses. Unit Load AGVs handle specific loads, moving discrete items efficiently between defined locations. Tugger AGVs, on the other hand, are specialized for towing carts filled with goods, optimizing transport in expansive facilities. Each type enhances logistics efficiency, reduces labor costs, and increases safety in consumer goods handling.</p></p>

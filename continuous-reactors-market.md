<p><p>The continuous reactors market includes various types designed for efficient chemical processing. Tubular reactors provide high temperature and pressure operations ideal for gas-phase reactions. Fixed bed reactors support solid catalysts and are often used in petroleum refining. Fluid bed reactors enhance mass and heat transfer for solid-liquid or gas-solid reactions. Continuous stirred tank reactors maintain uniform composition and temperature through continuous mixing, making them suitable for liquid-phase reactions. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, optimizing production efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>

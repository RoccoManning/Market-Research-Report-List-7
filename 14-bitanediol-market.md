<p><p>1,4 Butanediol (BDO) is produced through various processes, each catering to different market demands. The Reppe process utilizes acetylene and formaldehyde, while the Davy process employs hydrogenation of maleic anhydride. The Butadiene process transforms butadiene in the presence of hydrogen, and the Propylene oxide process derives BDO from propylene oxide. Each method offers distinct production efficiencies and costs, impacting the overall availability and pricing of BDO in the chemical industry.</p></p>

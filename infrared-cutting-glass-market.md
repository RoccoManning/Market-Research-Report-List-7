<p><p>The Infrared Cutting Glass market is categorized primarily into two types: Absorption Type and Reflection Type. Absorption Type glasses work by absorbing infrared radiation, converting it into heat, thereby preventing its passage. This type is often used in applications requiring thermal insulation. In contrast, Reflection Type glasses utilize reflective coatings to minimize infrared transmission, directing heat away from the surface. This type is ideal for energy-efficient applications in buildings and vehicles, enhancing comfort while reducing energy consumption.</p></p>

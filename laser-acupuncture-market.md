<p><p>The laser acupuncture market encompasses various types of laser sources used for therapeutic treatments. Carbon sources utilize a high-intensity beam for precise acupuncture stimulation. He-Ne light sources emit a low-level red light, beneficial for enhancing blood circulation and promoting healing. Semiconductor lasers offer versatility and are increasingly popular due to their compact size and efficiency. Nd:YAG lasers provide deep tissue penetration and are effective for more profound therapeutic applications, making them suitable for a wide range of acupuncture practices.</p></p>

<p><p>Heterojunction (HJT) solar panels utilize a combination of amorphous and crystalline silicon layers to enhance efficiency. The market is categorized into monofacial and bifacial cells. Monofacial HJT cells generate energy from one side, typically featuring a well-defined front surface. In contrast, bifacial HJT cells can capture sunlight from both sides, offering improved energy yield, especially in reflective environments. As demand for high-efficiency solar solutions grows, these technologies are increasingly favored for their performance and versatility in various applications.</p></p>

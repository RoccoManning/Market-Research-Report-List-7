<p><p>The healthcare packaging market consists of primary and secondary packaging types. Primary packaging directly contains and protects the product, ensuring product safety and integrity, which is crucial for pharmaceuticals and medical devices. This includes vials, blister packs, and syringes. Secondary packaging, on the other hand, groups primary packages for distribution and retail, enhancing branding and providing additional protection. This includes boxes, cartons, and labels, facilitating easier handling and storage while ensuring compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>

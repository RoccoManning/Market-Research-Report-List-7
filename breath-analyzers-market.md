<p><strong>The Breath Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fuel Cell</li><li>Semiconductor</li><li>Infrared</li><li>Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Breath analyzers are categorized into four main types: Fuel Cell, Semiconductor, Infrared, and Crystal. Fuel cell analyzers utilize electrochemical reactions to detect alcohol levels, providing accurate results. Semiconductor analyzers rely on gas sensors and are generally more affordable but less precise. Infrared analyzers measure specific gases through light absorption, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. Crystal-based analyzers use piezoelectric technology to analyze breath composition, suitable for detecting a range of compounds. Each type serves unique applications in health, law enforcement, and safety.</p></p>

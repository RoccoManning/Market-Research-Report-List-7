<p><strong>The Torque Limiter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Friction Plate</li><li>Magnetic Particle</li><li>Magnetic Hysteresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The torque limiter market features three primary types: friction plate, magnetic particle, and magnetic hysteresis. Friction plate limiters use friction to slip when a predetermined torque is exceeded, providing protection against overloads. Magnetic particle limiters utilize a magnetic field to control torque transmission, allowing slip at set levels for enhanced safety. Magnetic hysteresis limiters rely on magnetic materials to generate resistance, offering precise control over torque limits. These types cater to various industrial applications requiring reliable torque management and overload protection.</p></p>
